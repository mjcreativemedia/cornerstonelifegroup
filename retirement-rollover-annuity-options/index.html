Review before moving retirement money

A rollover decision can affect income, taxes, liquidity, fees, and long-term flexibility. Cornerstone Life Group can help begin the conversation around annuity options, fixed indexed annuities, and income strategies that may be available.

Annuities are about fit

Annuities can be useful for some conservative investors, retirees, and pre-retirees, but they are not right for everyone. A proper review should consider time horizon, liquidity needs, risk tolerance, income goals, and product terms.

A careful conversation

The first step is not a product pitch. It is a review of what you are trying to protect, what income you may need, and what concerns you have about market losses or retirement timing.

Annuity questions retirees ask

Can I roll over a 401(k) or IRA into an annuity?

It may be possible depending on the account, product, state, and suitability. Any rollover should be reviewed carefully before a decision is made.

Do annuities protect against market losses?

Some annuity products offer principal protection features, but guarantees depend on the claims-paying ability of the issuing carrier and the specific contract terms.
