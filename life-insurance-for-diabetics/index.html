Coverage may still be possible

Many people with Type 2 diabetes, controlled A1C history, or stable medication routines may still have life insurance options. Even if traditional coverage is difficult, simplified issue or final expense coverage may be worth reviewing.

Products often considered

Depending on your situation, simplified whole life, final expense, guaranteed issue, or term life may be considered. Every carrier reviews diabetes differently, so the right fit depends on your age, health, state, and underwriting answers.

Questions about diabetes and coverage

Will I be denied because I use medication?

Medication alone does not decide the outcome. Carriers may look at diagnosis age, control, complications, tobacco use, and other health history.

What if I was declined before?

A previous decline does not always end the conversation. Other carriers or product types may have different requirements.
